Coconut Logs

Description

A 2-ingredient snack that requires just 10 minutes out of your day and offers health benefits and taste until it lasts. Serve them as a quick dessert after dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup dates pitted
  • 1 cup desiccated coconut
  1. Place ingredients in a blender and blend until the two ingredients have combined.
  2. Roll into approximately 6 logs and enjoy straight away or store them in the fridge.
